# Frequently Asked Questions (`/frequently-asked-questions/`)

**Source URL:** https://craigmoor.com.au/frequently-asked-questions/

**Browser title:** Frequently Asked Questions – Craigmoor

**Short label in menus:** FAQs

---

#### Do I have to be a wine club member to buy wine?

You don’t have to join our wine club to purchase, but if you do, you will receive ongoing member discounts on all of our products.

Other Craigmoor Wine Club Member benefits include:

- Free Membership
- Free delivery
- Regular offers
- Flexibility – you choose when and what you would like to buy
- First access to new and limited release wines
- Email newsletters

With all of these benefits, there is no reason not to join!

#### How much does it cost for delivery?

If you are a wine club member, delivery is free within Australia. If you do not want to become a member, standard delivery charges may apply.

Please note that the minimum order quantities for delivery is 12 bottles.

#### When will I receive my delivery?

Depending on where you live, the delivery time will normally be between 2 and 14 business days. Your wine will be delivered by Australia Post in most instances.

#### I can’t have my wine delivered home – what else can I do?

We can also deliver wine to your work place. As we currently use Australia Post for deliveries, your wine can be delivered to a PO Box.

#### What happens if there is nobody around to accept my delivery?

Upon checkout, please be sure to select “Authority to Leave” and also leave clear delivery instructions, such as “Leave by the front door” or “leave at the back gate” when you place your order. In most cases, we can also deliver to your work address during standard business hours. If there is not anywhere safe to leave your order, it is best that you request to sign for the delivery. If you do this and you are not home, the courier will leave a calling card – you can then call to arrange a convenient time for delivery.

#### What happens if I don’t like the wine?

We guarantee the quality of our wines. If you try a wine and don’t like it, just contact us within 30 days of making your purchase and we will credit your account to the value of this wine – it is that easy.

#### What happens if some of my wine gets broken during delivery, or it is not delivered at all?

This is rarely ever a problem. However, if there is an issue with your delivery, we will replace or credit your account for anything you order but do not receive.
