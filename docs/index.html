The estate, in brief

Craigmoor is among Mudgee’s oldest vineyards — a touchstone for Australian chardonnay and a growing line-up of estate-grown reds and blends. Wines are offered through Oatley Fine Wine Merchants and the Craigmoor online cellar door.
