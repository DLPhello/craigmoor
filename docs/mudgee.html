CELLAR DOOR – MUDGEE NSW

Established in 1858 and surrounded by picturesque vines and gum trees on the banks of Pipeclay Creek, the Cellar Door, ‘Craigmoor’, is Mudgee’s oldest winery and vineyard.

Situated just 5 minutes’ drive from the centre of town, the property features picnic grounds around the Craigmoor Cricket Oval, the historic Craigmoor Winery and within it, our cellar door and wine museum.

A wide range of Craigmoor & Montrose wines are available for tasting and purchase at our Cellar Door. Taste one wine or taste them all with our personable and informative team.
